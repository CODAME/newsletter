Workshop: Data Visualization
Tracing the Patterns Around Us
with James Proctor
Data visualization is an important tool for describing and understanding our world, selves, and time. By asking questions, observing, and thinking critically we can surface dramatic qualities from massive data sets.
  • Hands-on guided tour of the complete data visualization process.
  • Identifying questions of interest, collect data from observation, and analyze what we find.
  • Develop your own story for use in a context of your choosing.
  • Under stand ethical and practical challenges in data collection and visualization.
