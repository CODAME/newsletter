ModBod Masterpieces
Derivative artwork by @uconko

Modbod is a dimensional photobooth. Visitors are 3d scanned and their likeness contributes to a catalog of over 700 models. The scans are Creative Commons By Attribution licensed, making them perfect for remix. Will you reuse them for training AI? Stylized renders? Video game assets? Interactive music "videos"?!

Submit your masterpiece to be shown in 『  S P  ▴  C E  』or be the seed of something new by adding your immortal image at the ART+TECH Festival 2019!

About CODAME
CODAME shapes the future through inspiring experiences and playful ART+TECH projects. We produce events, installations, and workshops as an arts collective. CODAME splits all proceeds with participating artists. We operate as nonprofit members of Intersections for the Arts, a 501(c)(3).
