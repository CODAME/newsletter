Workshop:
Immersive XR Journeys
Unity for artists with
Jasmine Roberts
Join creative technologist Jasmine Roberts to learn Unity Engine, used in 80% of AR/MR/VR projects. You'll explore concepts like scale and haptic interaction to build surreal immersive environments. Then using OpenXR you'll eaesily port your interactive creation to different headsets and devices.
Attendees can also have themselves 3d scanned and learn how to import themselves into Unity! Using provided shaders and scripts, you'll learn how to customize your selfie sculpture.
Participants will need their own computer with any version of Unity (including free Unity Personal).
