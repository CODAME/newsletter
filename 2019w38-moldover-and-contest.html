Workshop: Sound Space Music
Creating Aural Environments
with Moldover
This hands-on workshop explores Digital Signal Processing concepts, acoustic phenomena like feedback and reverberation, and how to organize audio processes to create fun, expressive, and surprising sound-spaces.
  • No prior experience with music or composition required.
  • Use your own laptop with a built-in microphone capable of running the demo version of Ableton Live
  • Design a unique sound-space that transforms ambient sounds into a custom, evolving, interactive sonic landscape.
  • Workshop will culminate in an exposition of creations, open for attendees of all workshops to enjoy!
