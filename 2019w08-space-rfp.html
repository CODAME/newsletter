Space is a canvas on which we write our stories, paint our dreams, and build our realities. Space is a province of the known...and the unknown. As the film camera captured the transformation of the modern world, so, too, do emerging digital media point their lenses toward the future. Through art, as we architect space, we envision the future.
The 2019 CODAME ART+TECH Festival explores the many facets of space. How do technologies like virtual reality allow new ways of living, being, playing, and moving in space?
How might robotic prosthetics extend our bodies in space? How do radical mapping projects redefine our relationships to our world, and to each other?
In this exhibition, we aim to expand our space and transcend borders. CODAME encourages participation from international artists!

About CODAME
CODAME shapes the future through inspiring experiences and playful ART+TECH projects. We produce events, installations, and workshops as an arts collective. CODAME splits all proceeds with participating artists. We operate as nonprofit members of Intersections for the Arts, a 501(c)(3).
